MediSlot serves as a technology platform that connects patients with healthcare providers. We do not provide medical treatment, diagnosis, prescriptions, or emergency medical services.

  4. 4. Appointment Booking

    MediSlot allows users to request and manage appointments with registered healthcare providers. Appointment availability depends on the doctor's schedule and may change without prior notice. A booking is considered confirmed only after confirmation is displayed by the system or the healthcare provider. Patients are expected to arrive on time for scheduled appointments. Doctors may reschedule or cancel appointments because of emergencies, personal reasons, or other unavoidable circumstances.

  5. 5. Cancellation and Rescheduling

    Users may cancel or reschedule appointments according to the policies established by the healthcare provider. Frequent cancellations, repeated no-shows, or misuse of the appointment system may result in restrictions or suspension of your account. Refunds, if applicable, are governed by the doctor's or hospital's cancellation policy.
